Key Features

Swipe to Translate, Sentence Alignment

Simply swipe any paragraph on a webpage—our system automatically detects complete sentences and inserts translations right below the original text. Enjoy smart segmentation and one-to-one alignment for seamless bilingual reading.

Double Tap for Word Lookup & AI Explanation

Double-tap any word and AI will analyze its meaning in context, prioritizing common phrases. Go beyond literal translations—understand usage, part of speech, example sentences, and collocations.

AI Pronunciation, Listen & Speak

Switch between AI-generated and native TTS voices. Listen to words or full sentences with natural pronunciation—improve your listening and speaking skills together.

Context Analysis, Phrase Priority

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I use the swipe translation feature?

Simply swipe a section of text on any webpage with your finger or mouse, and the app will automatically insert the translation below the original sentence. To remove a translation, just swipe left on the translated text.

Q2: How does double-tap word lookup ensure accurate explanations?

The app sends the word and its surrounding context to the AI backend, prioritizing multi-word phrases and providing the most contextually relevant meaning and examples—so you won't be misled by literal translations.

Q3: What are the limitations of the free version?

The free version allows a limited number of AI translations and 20 AI pronunciations per day. For details, see the in-app introduction. To lift the limits, upgrade to Plus or use native TTS mode.

Q4: Which languages are supported?

The initial version supports translation and explanation between over 20 languages, including Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and more. More language pairs will be added in future updates. The app automatically detects the target language and uses the best prompt template for high-quality results.

Q5: How can I view my history?

Tap the "History" icon at the bottom of the main screen to view all your translation and lookup records, grouped by date. Swipe left or right to delete individual records or view details.
